Customers

NO.

King Soopers has a "just say yes" policy, so thinking of a situation where I had to tell a customer no was a bit of a challenge. We do frequently have customers that are moving ask for us to save them boxes from the product. Unfortunately, we are not allowed to ave them for safety and sanitation reasons. I always start by apologizing and then explain the reasons why we cannot save them. I always offer that they can check back periodically because we recycle them throughout the day, as well as offer that they can always call before they come over to ask us if we have boxes. In general this satisfies the customer while still allowing us to follow procedure.

Are They Right?

Is the customer always right? No. Do we let them believe they are and treat them like they are? Yes. I believe that is what good customer service is, letting them feel like they are right. There are of course situations where wrong is wrong, but overall you are there to provide a service and letting them feel right is just a prt of that.
